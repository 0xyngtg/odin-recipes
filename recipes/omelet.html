Baked Denver Omelet by Odin

Description

My family loves omelets, and this baked Denver omelet recipe is quick and easy. The directions are for a Denver omelet but experiment with other ingredients to make your favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 Onion, chopped
  • 1/2 Green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 Cup chopped cooked ham
  • 8 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup milk
  • 1/2 Cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F

    2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 10-inch round baking dish.

  2. Fry the onion

    3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in ham; continue cooking until heated through, about 5 minutes.

  3. Beat eggs and milk

    4. Beat eggs and milk together in a large bowl; stir in Cheddar cheese and ham mixture. Season with salt and black pepper. Pour into the prepared baking dish.

  4. Bake and serve

    5. Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are browned and puffy, about 25 minutes. Serve warm.
