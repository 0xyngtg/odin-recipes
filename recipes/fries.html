Air Fryer Sweet Potato Fries by Odin

Description

Up your fry game by making delicious air fryer sweet potato fries at home. Seriously, there's no need to go to a restaurant to get crispy, sweet, and savory sweet potato fries when you have this easy 20-minute recipe. You'll be making these sweet potato fries to go with every meal, so you better save it now.

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium sweet potato, peeled
  • 1 Tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon pepper
  • 1/8 Teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 Teaspoon ground sweet paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)

  2. Season the Potato:

    3. Cut sweet potato into 1/2-inch wide fries. Place in a bowl with canola oil; toss to coat. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika; mix until all fries are evenly coated.

  3. Put on to air fryer:

    4. Working in batches if necessary, place an even layer of fries in the air fryer basket.

  4. Cook the potatos:

    5. Cook in the preheated air fryer until golden, about 10 minutes. Repeat to cook remaining fries.

  5. Serve:

    6. Serve hot and enjoy!
